Externship Opportunities


Externships are an alternative to Internships in the Industry. During externship, we work on similar projects as internships, except that we meet in SJSU. Silicon valley professionals mentor and guide the students during the projects.
If you are interested, please send your resume to TheOpClubSJSU@gmail.com by April 17th 2020

Supply Chain Management Software for Organic Agricultural (For undergraduates. Industry Sponsorship Available)

  • Project abstract: This project aims to build an app that helps automate the supply chain
    • for an Organic Agricultural Startup. They will have to work with the company to reduce inefficiencies and maximize yield.
  • Skill needed to study, but not limited:
    • There might be android app development as front end
    • This is full-stack development, but we have not chosen the framework.

    • Reducing error in the Pharmacy(For undergraduates. Industry Sponsorship Available)
  • Project abstract: this project helps pharmacists in reducing common human errors with the assistance of ai. For example, the pharmacists might give you the wrong number of pills.
  • Skill needed to study, but not limited:
    • This is general engineering project where electrical, mechanical and computer engineers can work together and build a product.

    • Building an Open Source Networking Lab @SJSU and carrying out research projects under the guidance of Prof. Young-hee Park (Suited for long-term master thesis/project, limited scholarships available)
  • Project abstract: Build an open source router/switch. This open source router uses merchant silicon and open SDKs(like OpenNSL, SAI). Open Network Operating System(NOS) (For example: Stratum, SONiC) choice is to be decided.
  • Skill preferred to study, but not limited:
    • C, C++ programming
    • Should have done a course in Networking

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Where do the projects take place?

    Due to COVID-19, we will meet on Zoom or Google Hangout or BlueJeans. If the shelter-in-place ends, we will meet regularly at MLK Library or in SJSU campus.

    How oftern will we meet?

    Summer extenships are eight hour per day with breaks. To accomodate Silicon Valley mentors who work during the week(Mon-Fri) we may skip a day or two during weekdays and meet on weekends.

    How can I sign up for the programs?

    To sign up for the program send your resume to TheOpClubSJSU@gmail.com.

    Do I get paid during Externships?

    No, Externships are very similar to unpaid internships. However we have limited financial grants available for the needy.
